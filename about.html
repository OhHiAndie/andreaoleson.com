For as long as I can remember, creativity has been a big part of who I am. From being a little kid and using markers to give my dolls rainbow hair colors, to discovering pixel art as a teen; I have always strived to make the world around me just a little bit cooler (and a lot more colorful!)

Now, I’m a graphic designer with a passion for creating brand identities. My inspiration comes from music, people’s stories, and art and pop culture around me. Some of my other creative outlets are drawing, painting, and doing makeup. I’m always ready to put on some music, and get to work on something awesome!
