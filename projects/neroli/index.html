Neroli Cosmetiqué

Neroli is a beauty brand, focused on clean and fresh skincare. Their range of products includes bath gels and body lotions, as well as facial scrubs, moisturizers, and other products for the most relaxing “at-home spa” experience.

The color story features toned down oranges and light, airy grays and white — making the brand look fresh and natural, and enhancing Neroli's image as a vegan, cruelty-free brand.

Client: Student work.
Scope: Brand development, print design, web design.
